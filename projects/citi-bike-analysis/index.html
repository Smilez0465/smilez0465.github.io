Citi Bike Usage Analysis

Tools Used: Power BI, Excel

📌 Objective

Analyze NYC Citi Bike usage patterns to uncover trends in rider behavior by time, location, and customer type.

🔍 Key Steps

  • Cleaned and transformed raw Citi Bike CSV data in Excel
  • Imported into Power BI and built interactive dashboards
  • Visualized peak hours, top stations, trip durations, and customer segments
  • Created slicers for gender, age group, and user type

📈 Outcome

Identified peak usage times and areas for potential bike reallocation. Dashboard supports operations and marketing decisions.
